About Us

"Mu Social Services Club" is a non-political organization. Metropolitan University Social Services Club consists of all the departments of the varsity. Teachers and students of all departments will be able to join this club through registration. The main purpose of the Mu Social Services Club is to protect the environment around us and to serve the people. This club organizes tree planting every year and distributes winter clothes to the needy people in winter. Also this club arranged Blood Donation and Food Distribution program every year of march in the theme of Independance Day.
